## Why Make It?

Homemade caramel sauce turns three everyday ingredients into a dessert topping without fillers.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup heavy cream

## Instructions

1. Melt sugar until amber.
2. Add butter carefully.
3. Stir in cream.
4. Cool before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
