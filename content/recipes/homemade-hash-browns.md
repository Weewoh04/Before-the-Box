## Why Make It?

Homemade hash browns are crispy, simple, and made from potatoes instead of a frozen bag.

## Ingredients

- 4 russet potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. Shred potatoes.
2. Rinse and dry thoroughly.
3. Heat butter in skillet.
4. Cook potatoes until golden and crispy.

## Storage

3 days refrigerated.
