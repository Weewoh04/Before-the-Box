## Why Make It?

Homemade breakfast sausage lets you season ground pork yourself without mystery fillers.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground pork
- 1 teaspoon sage
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Mix seasonings into pork.
2. Form patties.
3. Cook in skillet until browned.

## Storage

3 days refrigerated or 3 months frozen.
