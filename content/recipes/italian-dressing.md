## Why Make It?

Homemade Italian dressing is a quick jar dressing made with oil, vinegar, and herbs.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in jar.
2. Shake vigorously.
3. Serve immediately.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
