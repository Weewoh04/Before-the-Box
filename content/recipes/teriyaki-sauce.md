## Why Make It?

Homemade teriyaki sauce is sweet, savory, and easy to mix without bottled sauce additives.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 teaspoon ginger

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Simmer 10 minutes.
3. Cool before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
