## Why Make It?

Homemade taco ranch seasoning combines two favorite blends for quick dinners, dips, and snack mixes.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
- 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
- 1 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Mix thoroughly.
3. Store airtight.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
