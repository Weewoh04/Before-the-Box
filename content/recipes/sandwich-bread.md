## Why Make It?

Homemade sandwich bread is soft, simple, and made with ingredients you can actually recognize.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread flour
- 1 cup warm water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast

## Instructions

1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast.
2. Let sit 5 minutes.
3. Add butter, salt, and flour.
4. Knead 8 minutes.
5. Let rise 1 hour.
6. Shape into loaf.
7. Rise 30 minutes.
8. Bake at 350 F for 30 minutes.

## Storage

3-4 days at room temperature.
