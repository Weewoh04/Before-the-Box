## Why Make It?

Homemade buttermilk biscuits are flaky, tender, and made with a short ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 6 tablespoons cold butter
- 3/4 cup buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients.
2. Cut in butter.
3. Add buttermilk.
4. Fold dough gently.
5. Cut biscuits.
6. Bake at 425 F for 12-15 minutes.

## Storage

2 days at room temperature.
