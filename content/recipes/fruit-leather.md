## Why Make It?

Homemade fruit leather turns real fruit into a lunchbox snack without artificial colors or flavors.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups fruit
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Honey to taste, optional

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients until smooth.
2. Spread thinly on parchment-lined baking sheet.
3. Bake at 170 F for 4-6 hours.
4. Cut into strips and roll.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
