## Why Make It?

Homemade enchilada sauce is rich, simple, and ready without opening a can.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in saucepan.
2. Whisk in flour and spices.
3. Slowly add broth.
4. Simmer until thickened.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
