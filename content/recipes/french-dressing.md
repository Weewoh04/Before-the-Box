## Why Make It?

Homemade French dressing is a simple sweet-tangy dressing you can shake together in minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Shake well.
3. Refrigerate.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
