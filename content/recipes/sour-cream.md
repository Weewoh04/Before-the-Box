## Why Make It?

Homemade sour cream is an easy dairy swap with a short ingredient list and a fresh tangy flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4 cup milk

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Cover loosely.
3. Let sit at room temperature 24 hours.
4. Refrigerate before serving.

## Storage

7-10 days refrigerated.
