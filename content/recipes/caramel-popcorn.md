## Why Make It?

Homemade caramel popcorn is crunchy, sweet, and made from pantry basics instead of a snack bag.

## Ingredients

- 10 cups popped popcorn
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup corn syrup or honey
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Make caramel from butter, sugar, and syrup.
2. Stir in baking soda.
3. Coat popcorn.
4. Bake at 250 F for 45 minutes.

## Storage

1 week airtight.
