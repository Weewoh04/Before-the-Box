## Why Make It?

Homemade garlic herb seasoning is an all-purpose blend for roasted vegetables, pasta, chicken, and bread.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon parsley
- 1 teaspoon basil
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 teaspoon thyme

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients together.
2. Store in spice jar.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
