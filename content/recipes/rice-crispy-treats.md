## Why Make It?

Homemade rice crispy treats are a quick classic with a short, easy ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 6 cups crispy rice cereal
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 10 ounces marshmallows

## Instructions

1. Melt butter.
2. Add marshmallows until smooth.
3. Stir in cereal.
4. Press into pan.
5. Cool before cutting.

## Storage

5 days airtight.
