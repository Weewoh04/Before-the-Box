## Why Make It?

Homemade hamburger helper mix keeps a quick dinner shortcut on hand with ingredients you can recognize.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups elbow macaroni
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons dried milk powder
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To prepare, brown 1 pound ground beef.
4. Add mix and 3 cups water.
5. Cook until pasta is tender.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
