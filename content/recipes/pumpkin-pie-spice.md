## Why Make It?

Homemade pumpkin pie spice is easy to mix from warm baking spices you may already have.

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 2 teaspoons nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cloves

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
