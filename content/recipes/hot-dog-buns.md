## Why Make It?

Homemade hot dog buns are soft, simple, and made without the extra additives often found in packaged buns.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup warm milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Knead until smooth.
3. Rise 1 hour.
4. Shape into bun logs.
5. Rise 30 minutes.
6. Bake at 375 F for 12-15 minutes.

## Storage

3 days at room temperature.
