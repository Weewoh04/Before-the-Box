## Why Make It?

Homemade waffle mix makes weekend breakfasts faster while keeping the ingredient list simple.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To make waffles, combine 1 cup mix, 1 egg, 3/4 cup milk, and 2 tablespoons oil.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
