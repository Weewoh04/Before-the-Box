## Why Make It?

Homemade popsicles are a simple frozen snack you can make with fruit, yogurt, or juice.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups fruit
- 1/2 cup yogurt or juice

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients.
2. Pour into popsicle molds.
3. Freeze 6 hours.

## Storage

3 months frozen.
