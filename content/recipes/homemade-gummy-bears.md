## Why Make It?

Homemade gummy bears are a fun kid snack made with juice, honey, and gelatin.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup fruit juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons gelatin

## Instructions

1. Warm juice and honey.
2. Whisk in gelatin.
3. Pour into molds.
4. Refrigerate until firm.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
