## Why Make It?

Homemade cream of chicken soup replaces the can with a quick, creamy sauce made from familiar ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in saucepan.
2. Whisk in flour.
3. Slowly add broth and milk.
4. Simmer until thick.
5. Season to taste.

## Storage

4 days refrigerated.
