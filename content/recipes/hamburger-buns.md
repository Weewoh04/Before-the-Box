## Why Make It?

Homemade hamburger buns are soft, sturdy, and easy to make with basic baking ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup warm milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Knead dough.
3. Let rise 1 hour.
4. Divide into 8 portions.
5. Shape into buns.
6. Rise 30 minutes.
7. Bake at 375 F for 15 minutes.

## Storage

3 days at room temperature.
