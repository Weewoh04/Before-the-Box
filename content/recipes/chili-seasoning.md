## Why Make It?

Homemade chili seasoning is an easy pantry blend that lets you control the flavor and salt.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store in airtight container.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
