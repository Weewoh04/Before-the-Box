## Why Make It?

Homemade cornbread mix gives you the convenience of a box with ingredients you chose yourself.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cornmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To bake, combine 2 cups mix, 1 egg, and 1 cup milk.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
