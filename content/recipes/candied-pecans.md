## Why Make It?

Candied pecans are simple to make at home and perfect for snacks, salads, and holiday treats.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups pecans
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1 egg white

## Instructions

1. Beat egg white until frothy.
2. Toss pecans with egg white.
3. Add sugar and cinnamon.
4. Bake at 300 F for 30 minutes.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
