## Why Make It?

Homemade yogurt turns milk into a flexible staple for breakfasts, smoothies, sauces, baking, and snacks.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups whole milk
- 2 tablespoons plain yogurt with live cultures

## Instructions

1. Heat milk to 180 F.
2. Cool to 110 F.
3. Stir in yogurt.
4. Cover and keep warm 8-12 hours.
5. Refrigerate.

## Storage

7-10 days refrigerated.
