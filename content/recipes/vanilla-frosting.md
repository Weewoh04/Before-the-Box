## Why Make It?

Homemade vanilla frosting is creamy, simple, and skips the tub of shelf-stable extras.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Beat butter until creamy.
2. Add sugar gradually.
3. Add milk and vanilla.
4. Beat until fluffy.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.

## What's In The Box?

Homemade:
- Butter
- Sugar

- Hydrogenated oils
- Preservatives
- Artificial flavors
