## Why Make It?

Homemade cookie dough lets you bake fresh cookies anytime with familiar ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Cream butter and sugar.
2. Add egg.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Fold in chocolate chips.

## Storage

3 days refrigerated or 3 months frozen.
