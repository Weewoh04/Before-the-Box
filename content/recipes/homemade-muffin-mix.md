## Why Make It?

Homemade muffin mix turns basic pantry ingredients into quick breakfasts and snacks.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To make muffins, combine 2 cups mix, 1 egg, 3/4 cup milk, and 1/4 cup melted butter.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
