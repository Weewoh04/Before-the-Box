## Why Make It?

Homemade pie crust uses real butter and turns simple ingredients into a flaky baking staple.

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup cold butter
- 6 tablespoons ice water

## Instructions

1. Cut butter into flour.
2. Add water gradually.
3. Form dough.
4. Chill 30 minutes.
5. Roll and use.

## Storage

3 days refrigerated or freeze 3 months.
