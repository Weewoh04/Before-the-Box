## Why Make It?

Homemade whipped cream tastes fresher than the tub or can, and you can control the sweetness.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Chill bowl and beaters.
2. Beat cream until soft peaks form.
3. Add sugar and vanilla.
4. Beat until stiff peaks form.

## Storage

Up to 2 days refrigerated.
