## Why Make It?

Homemade cream of celery soup gives you a pantry-style shortcut with fresh celery and no mystery extras.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup celery, diced
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup broth

## Instructions

1. Cook celery in butter.
2. Stir in flour.
3. Add liquids.
4. Simmer until thick.

## Storage

4 days refrigerated.
