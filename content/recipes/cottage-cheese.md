## Why Make It?

Homemade cottage cheese turns milk into fresh curds with just vinegar and salt.

## Ingredients

- 1 gallon whole milk
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat milk to 190 F.
2. Stir in vinegar.
3. Let curds form.
4. Strain.
5. Rinse and salt.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
