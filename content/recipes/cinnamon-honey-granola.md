## Why Make It?

Cinnamon honey granola is a crunchy breakfast or snack made with pantry basics.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups rolled oats
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 cup melted butter

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Spread on baking sheet.
3. Bake at 325 F for 25 minutes.
4. Cool before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
