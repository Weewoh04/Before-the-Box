## Why Make It?

Homemade rice pilaf mix is an easy shelf-stable side dish without the boxed mix ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups long grain rice
- 1/2 cup broken spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon
- 1 teaspoon onion powder

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To prepare, cook 1 cup mix with 2 cups water.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
