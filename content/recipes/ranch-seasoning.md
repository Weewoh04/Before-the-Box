## Why Make It?

Homemade ranch seasoning keeps the familiar herby flavor without relying on packets.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- 1 tablespoon dried dill
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To make dressing, stir 1 tablespoon seasoning with 1 cup mayonnaise and 1/2 cup milk.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
