## Why Make It?

Homemade blackening seasoning adds smoky heat to fish, chicken, or vegetables without a store blend.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix spices thoroughly.
2. Use on fish, chicken, or vegetables.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
