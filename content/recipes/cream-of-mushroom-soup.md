## Why Make It?

Homemade cream of mushroom soup is a simple substitute for canned soup in casseroles and comfort food recipes.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 8 oz mushrooms, diced
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup broth

## Instructions

1. Saute mushrooms in butter.
2. Stir in flour.
3. Add milk and broth.
4. Simmer until thick.

## Storage

4 days refrigerated.
