## Why Make It?

Homemade apple pie spice is a fast baking blend for pies, oatmeal, muffins, crisps, and fall desserts.

## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoon allspice

## Instructions

1. Mix spices.
2. Store airtight.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
