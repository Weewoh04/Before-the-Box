## Why Make It?

Homemade ricotta is soft, fresh, and simple enough for pasta, toast, dips, and baking.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups whole milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Heat milk and salt.
2. Add lemon juice.
3. Let curds separate.
4. Strain 15 minutes.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
