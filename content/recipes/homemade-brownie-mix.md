## Why Make It?

Homemade brownie mix makes rich brownies easy while keeping the pantry label short.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 cup cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To bake, add 2 eggs and 1/2 cup melted butter.
4. Bake at 350 F for 25 minutes.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
