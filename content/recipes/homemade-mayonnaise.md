## Why Make It?

Fresh mayonnaise takes only minutes and contains ingredients you can actually recognize.

## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 cup light olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Add egg, lemon juice, mustard, and salt to a jar.
2. Pour oil on top.
3. Blend with an immersion blender until thick.
4. Store refrigerated.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
