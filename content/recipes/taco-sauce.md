## Why Make It?

Homemade taco sauce is a quick tomato-based sauce for tacos, burritos, bowls, and nachos.

## Ingredients

- 8 oz tomato sauce
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Simmer 10 minutes.
3. Cool before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
