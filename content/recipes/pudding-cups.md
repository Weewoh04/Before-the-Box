## Why Make It?

Homemade pudding cups are creamy, simple, and easy to portion for snacks.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Whisk ingredients together.
2. Cook over medium heat until thick.
3. Pour into containers.
4. Chill completely.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
