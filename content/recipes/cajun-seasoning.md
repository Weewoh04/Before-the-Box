## Why Make It?

Homemade Cajun seasoning gives you bold flavor without packets, fillers, or extra additives.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons paprika
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine all spices.
2. Mix thoroughly.
3. Store in airtight spice jar.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
