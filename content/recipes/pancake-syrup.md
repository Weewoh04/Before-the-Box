## Why Make It?

Homemade pancake syrup is quick, sweet, and easy to make when you need breakfast syrup in a pinch.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon maple extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and water.
2. Simmer 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Stir in extracts.

## Storage

1 month refrigerated.
