## Why Make It?

Homemade fajita seasoning keeps dinner quick while skipping the packet fillers.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix all spices together.
2. Store airtight.

## Storage

1 year pantry.

## What's In The Box?

Homemade:
- Chili powder
- Paprika
- Cumin
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder

Store Packet:
- Maltodextrin
- Silicon dioxide
- Modified food starch
