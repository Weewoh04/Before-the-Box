## Why Make It?

Homemade ketchup is a quick stir-together sauce with adjustable sweetness and no mystery ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 6 oz tomato paste
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Whisk until smooth.
3. Adjust sweetness to taste.

## Storage

3 weeks refrigerated.
