## Why Make It?

Homemade stir fry sauce is fast enough for weeknights and keeps bottled sauce extras out of dinner.

## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 teaspoon ginger

## Instructions

1. Whisk all ingredients together.
2. Use immediately or refrigerate.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
