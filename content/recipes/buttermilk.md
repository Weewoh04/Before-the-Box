## Why Make It?

Perfect when a recipe calls for buttermilk and you don't want to buy a whole carton.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar

## Instructions

1. Add lemon juice to milk.
2. Stir gently.
3. Let sit 10 minutes.
4. Use immediately.

## Storage

Use within 24 hours.
