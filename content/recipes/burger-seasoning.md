## Why Make It?

Homemade burger seasoning gives burgers big flavor with a small, recognizable ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder

## Instructions

1. Combine spices.
2. Sprinkle onto burgers before cooking.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
