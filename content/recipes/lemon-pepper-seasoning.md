## Why Make It?

Homemade lemon pepper seasoning adds bright flavor to chicken, fish, and vegetables without bottled blend extras.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons dried lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Mix thoroughly.
3. Store airtight.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
