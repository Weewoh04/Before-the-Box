## Why Make It?

Homemade sweet and sour sauce is a quick takeout-style sauce made from a few pantry staples.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup pineapple juice
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Instructions

1. Whisk ingredients together.
2. Simmer until thickened.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
