## Why Make It?

Homemade Italian seasoning lets you blend common dried herbs into one easy jar for pasta, sauces, soups, and roasted vegetables.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons basil
- 2 tablespoons oregano
- 1 tablespoon rosemary
- 1 tablespoon thyme
- 1 tablespoon parsley

## Instructions

1. Mix herbs together.
2. Store in spice jar.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
