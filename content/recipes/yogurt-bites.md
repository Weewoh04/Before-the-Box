## Why Make It?

Frozen yogurt bites are an easy kid snack made with yogurt, fruit, and a touch of honey.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 cup berries

## Instructions

1. Mix yogurt and honey.
2. Spoon onto parchment.
3. Top with berries.
4. Freeze until firm.

## Storage

2 months frozen.
