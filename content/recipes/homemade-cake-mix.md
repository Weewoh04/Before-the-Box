## Why Make It?

Homemade cake mix keeps a quick dessert option ready without boxed mix additives.

## Ingredients

- 5 cups flour
- 3 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
2. Store in airtight container.
3. To make cake, combine 3 cups mix, 1 cup milk, 2 eggs, and 1/2 cup butter.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
