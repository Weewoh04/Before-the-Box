## Why Make It?

Homemade chocolate syrup is rich, quick, and skips the squeeze-bottle ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cocoa powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Combine cocoa, sugar, and water.
2. Simmer 10 minutes.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Stir in vanilla.

## Storage

1 month refrigerated.
