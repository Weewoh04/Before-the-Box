## Why Make It?

Corn tortillas are one of the simplest from-scratch staples: masa harina, water, and salt.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups masa harina
- 1 1/2 cups warm water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Rest 10 minutes.
3. Form balls.
4. Press flat.
5. Cook 60 seconds per side.

## Storage

3 days refrigerated.
