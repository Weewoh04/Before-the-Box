## Why Make It?

Homemade crackers are crisp, simple, and easy to season your own way.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 cup water

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients into dough.
2. Roll very thin.
3. Cut into squares.
4. Bake at 400 F for 10-12 minutes.

## Storage

1 week in airtight container.
