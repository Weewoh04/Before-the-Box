## Why Make It?

Homemade marshmallow fluff is soft, sweet, and made from a handful of kitchen basics.

## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Heat sugar and water into syrup.
2. Beat egg whites to soft peaks.
3. Slowly add hot syrup.
4. Beat until fluffy.
5. Add vanilla.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
