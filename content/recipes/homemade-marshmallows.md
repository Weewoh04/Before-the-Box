## Why Make It?

Homemade marshmallows are soft, fluffy, and made without the mystery extras found in many packaged sweets.

## Ingredients

- 3 packets unflavored gelatin
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1 cup corn syrup or honey
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Bloom gelatin in 1/2 cup water.
2. Heat sugar, syrup, salt, and remaining water.
3. Bring to 240 F.
4. Slowly pour into gelatin while mixing.
5. Beat 10 minutes until fluffy.
6. Add vanilla.
7. Spread into greased pan.
8. Let set 6 hours before cutting.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
