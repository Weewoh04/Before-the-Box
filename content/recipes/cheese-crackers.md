## Why Make It?

Homemade cheese crackers are crisp, cheesy, and made with a short ingredient list.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients into dough.
2. Roll thin.
3. Cut into squares.
4. Bake at 350 F for 12-15 minutes.

## Storage

1 week airtight.
