## Why Make It?

Homemade brown sugar is useful when you run out, and it lets you control how light or dark the sugar tastes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon molasses

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and molasses.
2. Mix until evenly distributed.
3. Break up any clumps.

## Storage

Several months in airtight container.
