## Why Make It?

Homemade mozzarella is a hands-on dairy recipe that shows how milk can become a fresh, stretchy cheese.

## Ingredients

- 1 gallon whole milk
- 1 1/2 teaspoons citric acid
- 1/4 tablet rennet

## Instructions

1. Dissolve citric acid in water.
2. Add to milk and heat.
3. Add rennet.
4. Cut curds.
5. Heat and stretch curds.
6. Shape into balls.

## Storage

3-5 days refrigerated.
