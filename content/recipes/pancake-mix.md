## Why Make It?

Homemade pancake mix keeps breakfast quick without relying on boxed mixes.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store in airtight container.
3. To make pancakes, combine 1 cup mix, 3/4 cup milk, 1 egg, and 1 tablespoon melted butter.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
