## Why Make It?

Homemade granola bars are an easy no-bake snack with simple ingredients and flexible add-ins.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips, optional

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Press into lined pan.
3. Chill 1 hour.
4. Cut into bars.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
