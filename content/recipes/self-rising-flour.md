## Why Make It?

Homemade self-rising flour is a simple baking swap when a recipe calls for it and you only have all-purpose flour.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk ingredients together.
2. Use immediately or store.

## Storage

3 months pantry.
