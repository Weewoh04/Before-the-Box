## Why Make It?

Homemade stuffing mix gives you a fast holiday-style side dish from dried bread and simple seasonings.

## Ingredients

- 6 cups bread cubes
- 2 teaspoons parsley
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Store airtight.
3. To prepare, add 1 1/2 cups hot broth and 4 tablespoons butter.

## Storage

3 months pantry.
