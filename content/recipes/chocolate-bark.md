## Why Make It?

Homemade chocolate bark is an easy sweet treat you can customize with whatever nuts and fruit you like.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup nuts
- 1/4 cup dried fruit

## Instructions

1. Melt chocolate.
2. Spread on parchment.
3. Add toppings.
4. Chill until firm.
5. Break into pieces.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
