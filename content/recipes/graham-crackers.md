## Why Make It?

Homemade graham crackers are sweet, crisp, and perfect for snacks, lunchboxes, or homemade treats.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 cup honey
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Chill dough.
3. Roll thin.
4. Cut into rectangles.
5. Bake at 350 F for 12-15 minutes.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
