## Why Make It?

Homemade butter contains one ingredient: cream. That's it. It's a fun beginner recipe and a great way to learn how simple many grocery staples really are.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups heavy whipping cream

## Equipment

- Quart mason jar with lid
- Fine mesh strainer
- Bowl

## Instructions

1. Pour cream into mason jar, filling halfway.
2. Secure lid tightly.
3. Shake continuously for 5-10 minutes.
4. The cream will become whipped cream, then separate into butter and buttermilk.
5. Strain off buttermilk.
6. Rinse butter under cold water while pressing with a spoon.
7. Store in refrigerator.

## Storage

Refrigerator: 1-2 weeks
Freezer: 6 months
