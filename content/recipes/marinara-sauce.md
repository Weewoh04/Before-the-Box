## Why Make It?

Homemade marinara sauce is a simple tomato sauce for pasta, pizza, dipping, and batch cooking.

## Ingredients

- 28 oz crushed tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Saute garlic in olive oil.
2. Add tomatoes and seasonings.
3. Simmer 30 minutes.
4. Cool and store.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated or freeze for 3 months.
