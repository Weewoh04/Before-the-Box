## Why Make It?

Homemade BBQ sauce lets you control the sweetness, tang, smoke, and spice.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in saucepan.
2. Simmer 15 minutes.
3. Cool before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks refrigerated.
