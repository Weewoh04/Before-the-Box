## Why Make It?

Chia pudding is a make-ahead breakfast that turns simple ingredients into a creamy chilled jar.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup chia seeds
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
3. Stir before serving.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
