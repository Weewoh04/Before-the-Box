## Why Make It?

Homemade fruit snacks are a chewy treat made with fruit, gelatin, and honey.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup fruit puree
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons unflavored gelatin

## Instructions

1. Warm fruit puree.
2. Stir in honey.
3. Whisk in gelatin.
4. Pour into molds.
5. Refrigerate until firm.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
