## Why Make It?

Homemade pizza dough makes weeknight pizza flexible, inexpensive, and free from boxed crust mixes.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup warm water
- 2 teaspoons yeast
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Knead 8 minutes.
3. Rise 1 hour.
4. Shape pizza crust.
5. Add toppings.
6. Bake at 475 F for 12-15 minutes.

## Storage

Dough keeps 3 days refrigerated.
