## Why Make It?

Energy bites are a quick homemade snack with oats, peanut butter, honey, and a little chocolate.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Roll into balls.
3. Refrigerate 30 minutes.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
