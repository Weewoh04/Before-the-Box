## Why Make It?

Homemade Thousand Island dressing is creamy, tangy, and perfect for salads, burgers, and sandwiches.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- 1 teaspoon vinegar

## Instructions

1. Stir all ingredients together.
2. Chill before serving.

## Storage

1 week refrigerated.
