## Why Make It?

Homemade breakfast burritos are freezer-friendly and easy to batch for busy mornings.

## Ingredients

- 6 tortillas
- 6 eggs
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- 1/2 pound cooked sausage

## Instructions

1. Scramble eggs.
2. Combine with sausage and cheese.
3. Fill tortillas and roll.
4. Freeze individually.

## Storage

3 months frozen.
