## Why Make It?

Homemade applesauce is a simple kid-friendly snack made from apples, water, and optional cinnamon.

## Ingredients

- 6 apples, peeled and chopped
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon, optional

## Instructions

1. Add apples and water to a saucepan.
2. Simmer 20-25 minutes until soft.
3. Mash or blend to desired consistency.
4. Stir in cinnamon if desired.

## Storage

7 days refrigerated.
