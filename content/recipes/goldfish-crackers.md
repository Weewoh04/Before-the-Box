## Why Make It?

Homemade goldfish crackers bring the classic cheesy snack home with simple ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cheddar cheese
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients.
2. Roll thin.
3. Cut shapes.
4. Bake at 350 F for 12 minutes.

## Storage

1 week airtight.
