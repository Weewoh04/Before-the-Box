## Why Make It?

Homemade trail mix lets you choose the nuts, fruit, and chocolate without paying for a premade snack bag.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup almonds
- 1 cup cashews
- 1 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup sunflower seeds

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Store in airtight container.

## Storage

1 month pantry.

## What's In The Box?

Homemade:
- Nuts
- Raisins
