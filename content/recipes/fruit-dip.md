## Why Make It?

Homemade fruit dip is creamy, quick, and a simple way to turn fresh fruit into a snack plate.

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces cream cheese
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Beat ingredients together.
2. Chill before serving.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
