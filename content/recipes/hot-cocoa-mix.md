## Why Make It?

Homemade hot cocoa mix gives you a cozy pantry staple without artificial flavors or mystery stabilizers.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups powdered milk
- 1 cup cocoa powder
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
2. Store in airtight container.
3. To serve, add 1/4 cup mix to 1 cup hot water or milk.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
