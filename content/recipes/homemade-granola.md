## Why Make It?

Homemade granola is a simple breakfast staple with real oats, honey, and cozy flavor.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups rolled oats
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 F.
2. Mix all ingredients.
3. Spread on baking sheet.
4. Bake 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway.
5. Cool completely before storing.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
