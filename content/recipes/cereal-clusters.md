## Why Make It?

Homemade cereal clusters are crunchy, lightly sweet, and made without boxed cereal additives.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups rolled oats
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients.
2. Press onto baking sheet.
3. Bake at 325 F for 20 minutes.
4. Break into clusters after cooling.

## Storage

2 weeks airtight.
