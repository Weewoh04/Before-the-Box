## Why Make It?

Homemade alfredo sauce is rich, quick, and skips the jarred sauce stabilizers.

## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic

## Instructions

1. Melt butter.
2. Add garlic and cream.
3. Simmer 5 minutes.
4. Stir in cheese.

## Storage

4 days refrigerated.
