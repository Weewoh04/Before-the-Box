## Why Make It?

Homemade poultry seasoning is a simple herb blend for chicken, turkey, stuffing, and soups.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sage
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- 1 teaspoon rosemary
- 1 teaspoon marjoram
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix herbs together.
2. Store in spice jar.

## Storage

1 year pantry.
