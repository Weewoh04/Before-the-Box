## Why Make It?

Homemade instant oatmeal packets make busy mornings easy without boxed flavors or additives.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup quick oats
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon chia seeds
- Pinch cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients.
2. Store in individual jars or bags.
3. To prepare, add 3/4 cup hot water and stir.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
