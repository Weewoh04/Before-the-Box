## Why Make It?

Homemade breakfast sandwiches are easy freezer breakfasts you can assemble with ingredients you choose.

## Ingredients

- 6 English muffins
- 6 eggs
- 6 slices cheese
- 6 sausage patties

## Instructions

1. Cook eggs and sausage.
2. Assemble sandwiches.
3. Wrap individually.
4. Freeze for later.

## Storage

3 months frozen.
