## Why Make It?

Homemade flour tortillas are soft, flexible, and made with only a few pantry ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup lard or butter
- 1 cup warm water

## Instructions

1. Mix flour and salt.
2. Cut in fat.
3. Add water and form dough.
4. Rest 20 minutes.
5. Divide into balls.
6. Roll thin.
7. Cook on hot skillet 30 seconds per side.

## Storage

5 days refrigerated.
