## Why Make It?

Homemade peanut butter is rich, simple, and made with peanuts plus salt if you want it.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups roasted peanuts
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Process peanuts in food processor.
2. Continue blending until smooth.
3. Add salt if desired.

## Storage

1 month refrigerated.

## What's In The Box?

Homemade:
- Peanuts
- Salt

- Added oils
- Added sugar
- Stabilizers
