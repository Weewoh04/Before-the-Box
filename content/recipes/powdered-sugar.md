## Why Make It?

Homemade powdered sugar is a quick blender recipe for frosting, glazes, dusting, and baking.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch

## Instructions

1. Blend ingredients until powdery.
2. Store airtight.

## Storage

6 months pantry.
